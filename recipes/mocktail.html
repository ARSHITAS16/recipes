Citrus Mint Fizz Mocktail

DESCRIPTION

The Citrus Mint Fizz Mocktail is a vibrant and refreshing drink that combines the zesty flavors of fresh orange and lime juice with the cool, aromatic touch of mint. The addition of sparkling water adds a fizzy kick, making it the perfect thirst-quenching beverage for any occasion. Whether you're lounging by the pool or hosting a casual gathering, this mocktail brings a burst of citrusy goodness that everyone will enjoy.

This easy-to-make drink can be customized to your sweetness preference with a touch of honey or sugar, while the mint leaves elevate the flavor with a cool, fragrant twist. Itâ€™s a great choice for those who want a non-alcoholic, light, and flavorful drink without too much effort. Refreshing, flavorful, and beautifully garnished, the Citrus Mint Fizz is sure to be a crowd-pleaser!

INGREDIENTS

  • 1/2 cup fresh orange juice
  • 1/4 cup fresh lime juice
  • 1 tsp honey or sugar (optional)
  • 1/2 cup sparkling water or club soda
  • A few fresh mint leaves
  • Ice cubes
  • Orange or lime slices (for garnish)

STEPS

  1. In a small bowl or jar, muddle the mint leaves to release their aroma.
  2. In a glass, combine the fresh orange juice and lime juice.
  3. Add honey or sugar if you want a bit of sweetness (optional).
  4. Fill the glass with ice cubes.
  5. Garnish with orange or lemon slices and a mint leaf.
  6. Stir gently and serve cold.
